## Decision: **ACCEPT**

Dear Authors,

I am pleased to inform you that your manuscript has been accepted for publication in Physical Review B.

The revised manuscript addresses all concerns raised by the three referees in a thorough and scientifically rigorous manner. The addition of the test-set parity plot, the corrected ACE descriptor documentation, the DFT methods details, the new TCP phases table with verified space groups and Pearson symbols, and the quantitative SRO uncertainty bound all strengthen the paper substantially. The point-by-point response is clear, precise, and demonstrates a thorough understanding of the referees' concerns.

The editors are satisfied that the revised manuscript meets the standards of Physical Review B for novelty, rigor, and clarity.

**No further revisions are required.**

The manuscript will proceed to copyediting and will appear in an upcoming issue of Physical Review B.
