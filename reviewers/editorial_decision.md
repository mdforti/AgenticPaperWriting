# Editorial Decision

After considering the three referee reports, I find that the manuscript addresses a timely and potentially significant problem, but all referees identify substantive issues that must be resolved before the paper can be considered further. Reviewer 1 requests a clearer and more internally consistent account of the DFT provenance and a more quantitative external-validation presentation. Reviewer 2 finds the machine-learning workflow promising but asks for fuller reporting of MAE/$R^2$, the ensemble construction, and the phase-resolved complex-phase errors. Reviewer 3 is supportive of the thermodynamic direction but requires a more complete description of the Bragg--Williams/CEF treatment and a sharper quantitative comparison to experimental occupancies and known Fe--Mo phase stability.

The common theme is that the manuscript is conceptually strong but quantitatively under-documented in several central places. In particular, the use of schematic figure placeholders and the absence of a tabulated external-validation summary are not acceptable in a revised submission.

## Decision
Major Revision

Please submit a point-by-point response to all referee comments and revise the manuscript accordingly.
